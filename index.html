About Me

I am a research assistant (RA) at the Federal Reserve Board of Governors in Washington D.C., in the Division of International Finance. In June 2022, I graduated from UCLA with a B.S. in Mathematics/Economics and a Specialization in Computing.

My research interests include applied microeconomics (social safety net) and international finance (exchange rate crises).

My primary technical skills are Stata, Python, and HTML/CSS (I designed this website!).
